My Interests

A few of my favorite interests that I'll elaborate on are:

  • Sports
  • Recreation
  • Music
  • Technology

Sports: I love sports, basketball and soccer in particular, and would definitely consider myself a sports fan in a broad sense. I grew up playing soccer and I am definitely better at it than I am at basketball. My favorite teams are Atlanta United, Chelsea, and Barcelona. Basketball is something that I picked up a love for sometime late in high school. I never played as a kid and consequently have never been a member of an organized team. However, I very much enjoy playing pickup ball with friends or shooting around for fun. I follow the NBA very closely and my favorite basketball teams are the Minnesota Timberwolves and the Oklahoma City Thunder.

Recreation: My involvement in recreation is centered around outdoor activities. I enjoy mountain biking, climbing, kayaking, and hiking the most. I am a member of the climbing club at school. Mountain biking and kayaking are activities that I don't get to do as much because they are logistically more difficult and expensive to arrange but whenever I get the chance you can find me at the local trails or rivers.

Music: This one is a little bit more generic because who doesn't like music? But, it's still a big part of my daily life so I figured that I would include it. My favorite genres are alternative, hip-hop, and electronic. I enjoy going to music festivals and concerts of all sorts.

Technology: My final interest is perhaps the most broad of all the categories that I choose to include on this page. I play video games from time to time so I would consider it a casual hobby. I like reading and learning about new technology, which would explain my love for the Netflix series "Black Mirror".
